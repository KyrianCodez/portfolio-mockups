Our philosophy

We source every ingredient within 80 kilometres of our kitchen. The menu changes with what arrives each morning — sometimes a single perfect tomato rewrites the entire day's offering.

This is not a restaurant that serves what is easy. It is a place that serves what is right.

Our story

Born from a
love of quiet

Kissa began as a single table in a converted warehouse. The word itself — 喫茶 — means a place to drink tea, to sit, to exist without urgency. That spirit has guided every decision since the first morning we opened our doors.

We are not interested in volume. We are interested in the moment between the first bite and the second.
